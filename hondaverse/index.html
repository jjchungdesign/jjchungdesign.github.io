HONDAVERSE

In an ongoing partnership with Twitch, Honda wanted a creative way to promote the upcoming release of their latest vehicle, the Honda Prologue, the first mass-market fully electric vehicle. Channeling the ever-growing gaming community, Honda collaborated with Twitch to create the Hondaverse, an exclusive series of Fortnite maps featuring various Honda vehicles. The vehicle, as well as the exclusive map featuring the Prologue, were unveiled at TwitchCon 2023 for gamers and visitors alike.
