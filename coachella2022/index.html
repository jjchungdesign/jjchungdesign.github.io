Coachella 2022

Spotify hosted two experiences at Zenaya Estate over the weekend, working overnight to transition between the two events; SHM party and Desert Kickback.

Teamed up with Swedish House Mafia, the friday night party celebrated the release of new album along with their debut at Coachella main stage, with raving lights in sleek and elevated vibe over the estate with booze and hookah lounge.

The following day, tulum-inspired 'Desert Kickback' opened up for the guests to chill out, relax, recharge and reset ahead of the festival.
